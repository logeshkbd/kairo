Our Story

A little corner of Arabia,
plated in Coimbatore.

Kairo began with a simple obsession — mandi rice done the honest way. Long-grained, slow-cooked over gentle heat, absorbed with bone broth and spices carried from the Arabian Peninsula.

To that, we brought the fire of North Indian grills, the smoke of charcoal BBQ, and the bright accents of local Coimbatore flavour. The result is a menu that feels royal but never distant — sharing platters, velvet chickens, and combos that welcome students, families and friends alike.
