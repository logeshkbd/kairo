Our Culinary Journey

Serving Royal Arabic Hospitality in Coimbatore

Nestled near Sri Krishna College in Kuniyamuthur, Kairo Restaurant has become Coimbatore’s ultimate dining sanctuary for authentic Arabian Mandi and modern North Indian barbecue. We fuse slow-cooked royal traditions with contemporary flavors.

Every grain of our smoked basmati rice, every bite of our unique charcoal-grilled velvet chicken, and every refreshing sip of our signature floral drinks represents a passionate commitment to gastronomy. We offer a comfortable Arabian-styled retreat designed for families, friend groups, and students seeking generous portions without premium prices.
